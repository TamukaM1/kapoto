Values

Professional Conduct

You should expect from Kapoto employee's at all times to act in a professional manner, we believe professionalism demands courtesy and respect. You should expect punctuality, diligence in performance of duties, effectiveness in achieving objectives, discretion and ethical behavior.

Motivation

Our employees are selected on intelligence, aptitude, and the desire to obtain a career in law enforcement. Over the years we have found these individuals do their job well and with great success, it is not just a job for them, it is a crucial step in their law enforcement career.

Training

Kapoto maintains its own rigorous training program, each waiter applicant must complete and pass before working for us. Refresher courses are held on a regular basis to ensure each waiter is completely up to date and competent.
