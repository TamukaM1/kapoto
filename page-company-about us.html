  • What is Kapoto?
Kapoto Restaurant is Zimbabwe's leading kitchen for African meals. It provides healthy, delicious African meals at competitive prices.

INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND

*Kapoto was established in 2012 and within the short period of its existence, has been able to establish itself as a strong brand on the market. It is a top level restaurant targeting high income earners who value healthy eating. It is a family run business that strives to maintain the highest standards of quality not just in the dishes we serve, but also in the quality of our service and our overall image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Traditional Dishes Do You Offer

We are strictly a restaurant specializing in African dishes and have been firmed for developing our own unique Zimbabwe Traditional Cuisines that have been accepted across the racial lines.

Do you offer delivery services

Yes, We offer deliveries through local food delivery agents and very soon we will be launching our mobile app for orders and deliveries.
